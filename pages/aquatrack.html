Research and Inspiration

Water is the foundation of well-being—essential to every cell, every process in the body. Yet in fast-paced daily life, many people forget to hydrate, leading to fatigue, tension, and imbalance. AquaTrack was born from the idea that hydration should be mindful, personalized, and beautifully simple.

Taking inspiration from the natural world, from the steady flow of rivers to the freshness of morning dew, AquaTrack uses soft greens and fluid design to promote calm and clarity. This is more than just a tracker—it's a gentle reminder to care for yourself, one sip at a time.

Persona

The AquaTrack user is someone who values wellness and wants to improve daily habits without pressure. Whether they are busy professionals, fitness enthusiasts, or simply curious about self-care, AquaTrack supports them in building healthier hydration routines—gently and consistently.

Their motivation is to feel better, clearer, and more energized through something as simple as water—guided by intuitive design and natural cues.

User Journey

  • From the first screen, AquaTrack welcomes users into a soothing environment of greens and flowing shapes. The journey begins with setting hydration goals and moves through reminders, progress visualizations, and positive reinforcement.

  • The tone stays calm, non-intrusive, and encouraging—making hydration a habit that feels good.

User Storyboard

  • The storyboard illustrates:

  • - a user realizing they often forget to drink, then finding joy in meeting hydration goals

  • - a user noticing increased energy and clarity thanks to daily water tracking

User Stories

  • The storyboard illustrates:

  • - "As a busy worker, I want a simple reminder system, so I can stay hydrated during my workday."

  • - "As someone focused on wellness, I want to visualize my hydration trends, so I can stay motivated and balanced."

  • - "As a minimalist, I want an app that feels calming and uncluttered, so I enjoy returning to it every day."

UI-kit

The UI-kit for AquaTrack reflects the harmony of water and wellness—clean typography, fluid progress bars, soft greens, and gentle animations. It supports a calm user experience and consistent visual identity across the app.
