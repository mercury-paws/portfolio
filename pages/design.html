Persona

Creating a user persona helps define the needs, goals, and frustrations of the target audience, ensuring a more user-centered design. By crafting a detailed persona, we can better understand the real-life challenges users face and tailor solutions that truly benefit them. This approach enhances usability, engagement, and overall satisfaction with the product.

User Journey

Mapping the user journey helps visualize the steps a user takes to achieve their goal, highlighting pain points and opportunities for improvement. By understanding their experience, we can design a more intuitive and seamless interaction. This process ensures that the product effectively meets user needs at every stage.

User Storyboard

Creating a user storyboard brings the user journey to life by illustrating key interactions and emotions throughout their experience. This visual approach helps identify challenges and design solutions that align with real user needs. Storyboarding ensures a more engaging and user-friendly product.

User Stories

As a working student, I want the medical app to be fast and always accessible, so I don't waste time finding appointments.

As a mom and working professional, I want quick access to our medical info for efficient health management.

As a businessman, I want the medical app to remind me of my appointments, so I don't have to keep track of them.

Symbol

The goal was to represent care, wellness, and holistic well-being in a minimal yet impactful way.

A human figure symbolizes the doctor-patient relationship, care, and support.
