Research and Inspiration

Traditional Ukrainian embroidery—vyshyvanka—is rich in symbolism, healing intention, and cultural identity. Patterns once stitched by hand carried protective meanings, blessings, and stories of place and people. Inspired by this heritage, the Vyshyvanka Vibes app bridges ancient wisdom with modern emotional design.

Just like the soothing presence of a cat, the intricate beauty of vyshyvanka can foster a sense of calm, belonging, and inner strength. The app explores how traditional motifs, colors, and rhythm of pattern can be used to support emotional well-being and cultural mindfulness in a digital experience. It's not just a celebration of embroidery—it's a space for education, reflection, and connection through design.

Persona

Understanding our user means understanding their emotional and cultural needs. Whether they are diaspora Ukrainians seeking reconnection, artists looking for symbolic inspiration, or people interested in embroidery for mindfulness—the app provides personal meaning, creative freedom, and a sense of cultural continuity.

Each persona is driven by a desire to slow down, reconnect with roots, and find comfort in intentional, beautiful details.

User Journey

  • From the moment users open the web, they are invited into a visual world of warmth and tradition. The journey moves from exploration of patterns, to learning their meaning.

  • The emotional tone remains grounded, gentle, and human.
